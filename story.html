The Story Behind Overtime

In a world that moves too fast, Overtime was born from a simple thought — what if there was a place where time could slow down?

Designed with a blend of modern elegance and a touch of cosmic inspiration, Overtime is more than just a restaurant. It is a space where the noise fades, the lights feel softer, and every moment becomes something to truly experience.

From carefully crafted dishes to a calming atmosphere, every detail is created to give you a sense of comfort, warmth, and escape from the everyday rush.

“Take a break. Taste the moment. Stay a little longer.”

Whether you're sharing laughter with friends, spending time with family, or simply enjoying a quiet meal alone — Overtime invites you to pause, breathe, and make every second count.

Because here, it’s not just about food… it’s about the time you spend.
