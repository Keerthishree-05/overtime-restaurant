About Us

Overtime is a modern restaurant offering a comfortable and stylish dining experience with quality food and ambience, inspired by the vast beauty of space.

Here, guests don’t just dine — they embark on a journey through the cosmos. From ambient lighting that mimics distant galaxies to an atmosphere that feels like floating among the stars, every detail is designed to create a truly out-of-this-world experience.

At Overtime, you can relax, connect, and enjoy your meals as if you were dining in space itself — where time slows down, and every moment feels infinite.
